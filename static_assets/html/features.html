Features.

Tutoring Freedom
Mathboard now gives the freedom of moving teaching outside of the classroom. An unlimited number of students can join a teacher's session to begin learning.

Collaborative Learning
Use our chat function to communicate with your peers or the session's teacher, asking questions will always help to clarify concepts that may be difficult to understand.

Save Session
Never lose your Mathboard after a long session. Under the heading, 'Canvas' press 'Save' in order to download your very own copy, that you may want to distribute to your students.

Wolfram Alpha
Enter your mathmatical terms to have them automatically evaluated and converted into mathmateical notation that is easy to read and manipulate for all.

Quick Background Colour Change
Under the heading, 'Toggle' press 'Background' to change background to black, this is very useful for the productivity of some learners.

Draw Tool
When active, the draw tool allows you to start drawing on the canvas. You are given multiple options such as thickness of line and the freedom of picking any colour that may appeal to you. Save your favourite colours for use at another time!

Object Removal
Instead of just an eraser, Mathboard makes every line you draw into a new object, this can be selected individually or as a group by dragging over them. To delete objects under the heading, 'Object' press 'Delete'.
