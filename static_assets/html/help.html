How does Mathboard work?

Click on the type of user you are for a guide on using Mathboard.

Student

To begin you must enter your name into the given box, then press 'Join'.

A callout will appear on your screen. You must enter the room number given to you by your teacher, then press 'Join'.

You will now enter the session with your teacher and peers.

Press 'Chat' to begin collaborating with the teacher or other students, alternativley to leave the room press 'Leave Room'.


Teacher

You must first begin by starting a 'New Room'.

Next you must next give your students the session ID so that they can access your private Mathboard.

Now start your lesson, get creative!

Press 'Chat' to begin collaborating with students, when complete to end the session the room press 'Leave Room'.

Don't forget to save your canvas if you wish!
